Who are we?

At Trifork, we craft software for organizations ranging from start-ups to governments.

We see software as part of a package: we work closely with our customers to understand their business needs, and provide software alongside advice, support, and training.

Our major customers are in the educational, finance, healthcare, manufacturing and telecom industries.
